Privacy and Participant Information

This page provides basic public information for a demo-only messaging number associated with a university-affiliated cybersecurity research study demonstration.

Who operates this demo site

This site is operated by Jerson Francia with faculty oversight from Dr. Ben Schooley, in connection with Brigham Young University affiliated study preparation and demonstration activities.

Why this number exists

The associated toll-free number is intended for limited pilot use, infrastructure testing, and controlled demonstration messaging related to a cybersecurity research study.

It is not presented here as the final production messaging route for the full study.

Information involved in the demo

Demo communications may involve operational information such as phone numbers, message delivery status, message timing, and link-click events used to validate the messaging infrastructure.

  • This public webpage does not provide a sign-up form or collect direct submissions.
  • This public webpage does not request passwords, payment details, or sensitive credentials.
  • Questions about the demo may be directed through the public contact information below.

No public enrollment on this website

This website is informational only. It is not used to publicly enroll participants into a research study. Any future study participation would be handled through separate approved research procedures.
