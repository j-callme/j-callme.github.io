1. Introduction

These notes aim to be readable like a short research paper, while staying practical for content creation. We keep a single-column layout, quiet colors, and generous spacing, so the math and graphs are the focus.

2. Terminology & Assumptions

  • AD — Attack Damage
  • BAT — Base Attack Time
  • AS — Attack Speed multiplier (unitless)
  • AF — Attack speed factor used in later derivations

Note: exact in-game rounding, batching, and animation timing details will be documented alongside the math when we enable equations.
